## Getting Started

### What is the Job Aggregation Platform?

Our platform combines jobs from multiple sources including:
- **Direct employer posts**: Jobs posted directly by companies
- **LinkedIn**: Jobs from LinkedIn's job board
- **Indeed**: Jobs from Indeed's database
- **Naukri**: Jobs from Naukri.com
- **Monster**: Jobs from Monster.com

We automatically remove duplicates and rank jobs by quality, so you see the best opportunities first.

### Job Types We Support

- Full-time positions
- Part-time positions
- Contract work
- Freelance opportunities
- Internships
- Fellowships
- Academic positions

## Creating an Account

### Step 1: Register

1. Go to the homepage
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right
3. Select "Job Seeker"
4. Fill in your details:
   - Email address
   - Password (must be strong)
   - Full name
   - Phone number (optional)
5. Click "Create Account"

### Password Requirements

Your password must have:
- At least 8 characters
- One uppercase letter
- One lowercase letter
- One number
- One special character (!, @, #, $, etc.)

**Example**: `MyJob2024!`

### Step 2: Verify Your Email

1. Check your email inbox
2. Click the verification link
3. You're ready to start searching!

## Searching for Jobs

### Basic Search

1. Enter keywords in the search bar (e.g., "Python Developer")
2. Click "Search" or press Enter
3. Browse the results

### Advanced Filters

Use filters to narrow your search:

#### Location Filter
- Enter city, state, or country
- Example: "San Francisco, CA"

#### Remote Jobs
- Toggle "Remote Only" to see only remote positions

#### Job Type
Select one or more:
- Full-time
- Part-time
- Contract
- Freelance
- Internship
- Fellowship
- Academic

#### Experience Level
- Entry level
- Mid level
- Senior
- Lead
- Executive

#### Salary Range
- Set minimum salary
- Set maximum salary
- Filter by currency (USD, EUR, etc.)

#### Posted Date
- Last 24 hours
- Last 7 days
- Last 14 days
- Last 30 days

#### Job Source
- Direct posts (posted by employers)
- URL imports (imported by employers)
- Aggregated (from LinkedIn, Indeed, etc.)

### Search Tips

**Use specific keywords**:
- ✅ "Senior Python Developer Django"
- ❌ "Job"

**Combine filters**:
- Search: "Data Scientist"
- Location: "New York"
- Remote: Yes
- Salary: $100,000+

**Save your searches**:
- Bookmark search URLs to quickly return to them

### Understanding Search Results

Jobs are sorted by:
1. **Featured jobs** (marked with ⭐) appear first
2. **Quality score** (higher quality jobs rank higher)
3. **Posted date** (newer jobs rank higher)

**Quality indicators**:
- 🟢 High quality (90-100): Complete information, direct posts
- 🟡 Medium quality (70-89): Good information, some details missing
- 🟠 Lower quality (50-69): Basic information, aggregated posts

- Job type (full-time, part-time, etc.)

**Job Description**:
- Full description

**Application Information**:
- How to apply

### Job Sources

**Direct Posts** (🏢):
- Posted directly by the employer
- Apply through our platform
- Track your application status

**Aggregated Jobs** (🔗):
- Collected from other job boards
- Click "Apply on [Source]" to apply
- Opens the original job posting

**URL Imports** (📎):
- Imported by employers from other platforms
- May have application instructions in description

## Applying to Jobs

### Applying to Direct Posts

Direct posts allow you to apply through our platform:

#### Step 1: Prepare Your Application

Before applying, have ready:
- **Resume** (PDF, DOC, or DOCX, max 5MB)
- **Cover letter** (optional but recommended)

#### Step 2: Submit Application

1. Click "Apply Now" on the job detail page
2. Upload your resume
3. Write a cover letter (optional)
4. Review your application
5. Click "Submit Application"

#### Step 3: Confirmation

You'll see a confirmation message and receive an email confirmation.

### Applying to Aggregated Jobs

For aggregated jobs:

1. Click "Apply on [Source]" button
2. You'll be redirected to the original job posting
3. Follow the employer's application instructions

### Application Tips

**Resume Tips**:
- Use a clear, professional format
- Tailor your resume to the job
- Highlight relevant experience
- Keep it to 1-2 pages
- Save as PDF for best compatibility

**Cover Letter Tips**:
- Address the hiring manager by name if possible
- Explain why you're interested in the role
- Highlight 2-3 relevant achievements
- Keep it concise (3-4 paragraphs)
- Proofread carefully

## Tracking Your Applications

### My Applications Page

View all your applications in one place:

1. Click your profile icon
2. Select "My Applications"
3. See all applications with status

### Application Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 📝 Submitted | Application received by employer |
| 👀 Reviewed | Employer has viewed your application |
| ⭐ Shortlisted | You're being considered for interview |
| ❌ Rejected | Application not selected |
| ✅ Accepted | Offer extended or interview scheduled |

### What to Do Next

**Submitted**:
- Wait for employer to review
- Continue applying to other jobs

**Reviewed**:
- Employer is considering your application
- Be ready for a call or email

**Shortlisted**:
- Prepare for interview
- Research the company
- Practice common interview questions

**Rejected**:
- Don't be discouraged
- Apply to more positions
- Consider feedback if provided

**Accepted**:
- Congratulations!
- Respond promptly to the employer
- Prepare for next steps

## Tips for Success

### Job Search Strategy

**1. Apply Early**
- Jobs posted in the last 24-48 hours get the most attention
- Use the "Posted within" filter to find fresh opportunities

**2. Apply Often**
- Job searching is a numbers game
- Apply to 5-10 jobs per day
- Quality over quantity, but don't be too selective

**3. Customize Applications**
- Tailor your resume for each job
- Mention specific requirements from the job description
- Show you've researched the company

**4. Follow Up**
- For direct posts, check application status regularly
- If no response after 2 weeks, consider following up

**5. Stay Organized**
- Track which jobs you've applied to
- Note application dates
- Set reminders for follow-ups

### Profile Optimization

**Keep Your Profile Updated**:
- Update your resume regularly
- Add new skills and experience
- Keep contact information current

**Professional Email**:
- Use a professional email address
- Check email daily for responses
- Respond promptly to employer messages

### Interview Preparation

**Research the Company**:
- Visit their website
- Read recent news
- Understand their products/services

**Prepare Questions**:
- Ask about team structure
- Inquire about growth opportunities
- Understand the role's challenges

**Practice Common Questions**:
- "Tell me about yourself"
- "Why do you want this job?"
- "What are your strengths/weaknesses?"

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is the platform free to use?**

A: Yes! Job seekers can use all features for free, including searching, applying, and tracking applications.

**Q: How often are new jobs added?**

A: We aggregate jobs every 6 hours from our sources. Direct employer posts appear immediately.

**Q: Can I apply to the same job multiple times?**

A: No, you can only submit one application per job. Make sure your first application is your best!

**Q: How do I know if my application was received?**

A: You'll receive an email confirmation immediately after submitting. Check "My Applications" to track status.

### Account Questions

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**

A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset instructions.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**

A: Yes, go to Profile Settings and update your email. You'll need to verify the new email.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**

A: Go to Profile Settings → Privacy → Delete Account. Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

### Application Questions

**Q: What file formats are accepted for resumes?**

A: We accept PDF, DOC, and DOCX files up to 5MB.

**Q: Can I edit my application after submitting?**

A: No, applications cannot be edited after submission. Double-check before clicking "Submit"!

**Q: Why can't I apply to some jobs?**

A: Aggregated jobs require you to apply on the original platform. Click "Apply on [Source]" to be redirected.

**Q: How long do employers take to respond?**

A: Response times vary. Most employers review applications within 1-2 weeks. Some may take longer or not respond at all.

### Search Questions

**Q: Why am I seeing duplicate jobs?**

A: We automatically remove duplicates, but some may slip through. Report duplicates using the "Report" button.

**Q: What does "quality score" mean?**

A: Quality score indicates how complete and reliable a job posting is. Higher scores mean more detailed, verified information.

**Q: Can I save jobs to apply later?**

A: Currently, we don't have a "save" feature. Bookmark job URLs in your browser to return to them later.

**Q: Why don't all jobs show salary information?**

A: Employers aren't required to post salary ranges. We show salary when provided by the employer or source.

### Technical Questions

**Q: The website isn't working properly. What should I do?**

A: Try these steps:
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Contact support if the issue persists

**Q: Is my data secure?**

A: Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. We never share your personal information without permission.

**Q: Can I use the platform on mobile?**

A: Yes! Our platform is fully responsive and works on all devices.
