## General Questions

### What is the Job Aggregation Platform?

The Job Aggregation Platform is a hybrid job marketplace that combines automated job aggregation from multiple sources (LinkedIn, Indeed, Naukri, Monster) with direct employer posting capabilities. We help job seekers find opportunities and help employers reach qualified candidates.

### Is the platform free to use?

- **For Job Seekers**: Yes, completely free! Search, apply, and track applications at no cost.
- **For Employers**: We offer a free tier (3 posts/month) and paid plans for higher volume hiring.

### What makes this platform different from other job boards?

1. **Multiple Sources**: We aggregate jobs from LinkedIn, Indeed, Naukri, and Monster in one place
2. **Smart Deduplication**: We automatically remove duplicate listings
3. **Quality Scoring**: Jobs are ranked by quality and relevance
4. **Flexible Posting**: Employers can post directly or import from other platforms
5. **Application Tracking**: Built-in tools for managing applications (Basic/Premium tiers)

### What types of jobs are available?

- Full-time positions
- Part-time positions
- Contract work
- Freelance opportunities
- Internships
- Fellowships
- Academic positions

### How often are new jobs added?

- **Aggregated jobs**: Updated every 6 hours from external sources
- **Direct posts**: Appear immediately when employers publish them
- **URL imports**: Processed within 1-2 minutes

## For Job Seekers

### How do I create an account?

1. Click "Sign Up" in the top right
2. Select "Job Seeker"
3. Enter your email, password, full name, and phone
4. Verify your email
5. Start searching!

### Do I need an account to search for jobs?

No! You can search and view jobs without an account. However, you need an account to:
- Apply to direct posts
- Track your applications
- Save your preferences

### How do I search for jobs?

Use the search bar and filters:
- **Keywords**: Enter job title, skills, or company name
- **Location**: Specify city, state, or country
- **Remote**: Toggle for remote-only jobs
- **Job Type**: Filter by full-time, part-time, contract, etc.
- **Experience Level**: Entry, mid, senior, lead, executive
- **Salary Range**: Set minimum and maximum salary
- **Posted Date**: Last 24 hours, 7 days, 14 days, 30 days

### What does the quality score mean?

Quality score (0-100) indicates how complete and reliable a job posting is:
- **90-100** (🟢): High quality - Complete information, direct posts
- **70-89** (🟡): Good quality - Most details provided
- **50-69** (🟠): Basic quality - Some information missing

Higher scores mean more detailed, verified information.

### What's the difference between direct posts and aggregated jobs?

**Direct Posts** (🏢):
- Posted directly by employers on our platform
- Apply through our platform
- Track application status
- Employer can see your application

**Aggregated Jobs** (🔗):
- Collected from other job boards (LinkedIn, Indeed, etc.)
- Click "Apply on [Source]" to apply
- Opens original job posting
- Cannot track status on our platform

### How do I apply to jobs?

**For Direct Posts**:
1. Click "Apply Now" on job detail page
2. Upload your resume (PDF, DOC, or DOCX, max 5MB)
3. Write a cover letter (optional)
4. Click "Submit Application"

**For Aggregated Jobs**:
1. Click "Apply on [Source]" button
2. You'll be redirected to the original posting
3. Follow that platform's application process

### Can I edit my application after submitting?

No, applications cannot be edited after submission. Make sure to review carefully before clicking "Submit"!

### How do I track my applications?

1. Click your profile icon
2. Select "My Applications"
3. View all applications with current status

**Application Statuses**:
- 📝 **Submitted**: Application received
- 👀 **Reviewed**: Employer viewed your application
- ⭐ **Shortlisted**: Being considered for interview
- ❌ **Rejected**: Not selected
- ✅ **Accepted**: Offer extended or interview scheduled

### Why can't I apply to some jobs?

Aggregated jobs require you to apply on the original platform. Click "Apply on [Source]" to be redirected to the job posting.

### How long do employers take to respond?

Response times vary by employer. Most review applications within 1-2 weeks. Some may take longer or not respond at all. We recommend:
- Continue applying to other jobs
- Follow up after 2 weeks if no response
- Don't wait for one application - apply to multiple positions

### Can I save jobs to apply later?

Currently, we don't have a built-in "save" feature. You can:
- Bookmark job URLs in your browser
- Take notes of job IDs
- Apply as soon as you find interesting opportunities

### What file formats are accepted for resumes?

We accept:
- PDF (.pdf)
- Microsoft Word (.doc, .docx)
- Maximum file size: 5MB

**Tip**: PDF is recommended for best compatibility.

## For Employers

### How do I create an employer account?

1. Click "Sign Up" in the top right
2. Select "Employer"
3. Enter your email, password, company name, website, and description
4. Verify your email
5. Complete your company profile
6. Start posting jobs!

### What subscription tiers are available?

| Feature | Free | Basic ($49/mo) | Premium ($199/mo) |
|---------|------|----------------|-------------------|
| Monthly Posts | 3 | 20 | Unlimited |
| Featured Posts | 0 | 2 | 10 |
| Application Tracking | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Analytics | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Listing Duration | 30 days | 60 days | 90 days |

### How do I post a job?

**Option 1: Direct Posting**
1. Go to "Post a Job"
2. Fill in job details (title, description, requirements, etc.)
3. Review and publish

**Option 2: URL Import**
1. Go to "Post a Job" → "Import from URL"
2. Paste job URL from LinkedIn, Indeed, Naukri, Monster, or Glassdoor
3. Review imported details
4. Edit if needed and publish

### What is URL import?

URL import lets you quickly add existing job postings from other platforms. We automatically scrape the job details and create a listing on our platform.

**Supported platforms**:
- LinkedIn
- Indeed
- Naukri
- Monster
- Glassdoor

### How do featured listings work?

Featured listings:
- ⭐ Appear at the top of search results
- 🎯 Get 3-5x more views
- 💼 Stand out with special badge
- 📈 Higher application rates

**How to feature**:
- Check "Feature this job" when posting
- Or click "Feature" on existing job in "My Jobs"

**Quota**: Basic tier gets 2/month, Premium gets 10/month

### Can I edit a job after posting?

Yes! Edit anytime without consuming additional quota:
1. Go to "My Jobs"
2. Click on the job
3. Click "Edit"
4. Make changes and save

### How do I manage applications?

**Note**: Application tracking requires Basic or Premium tier.

1. Go to "Applications" in your dashboard
2. View all applications or filter by job
3. Click on an application to see details
4. Update status and add notes

**Application statuses**:
- Submitted → Reviewed → Shortlisted → Accepted
- Or: Submitted → Reviewed → Rejected

### How do I contact applicants?

Use the contact information provided in their resume. We don't provide built-in messaging. Contact applicants via:
- Email (from resume)
- Phone (from resume)
- LinkedIn (if provided)

### What happens when my job expires?

- Job is removed from search results
- Marked as "Expired" in your dashboard
- Applications are preserved
- You can reactivate anytime

**To reactivate**:
1. Go to "My Jobs" → Filter by "Expired"
2. Click on the job
3. Click "Reactivate"
4. Set new expiration date

### How do I delete a job?

1. Go to "My Jobs"
2. Click on the job
3. Click "Delete"
4. Confirm

**Warning**: Deleted jobs cannot be recovered. Applications are archived.

### What analytics are available?

**Premium tier only**:
- Total views and applications
- Views/applications per day charts
- Conversion rate (% of viewers who applied)
- Source breakdown (where applicants found your job)
- Comparison to industry benchmarks

### Can multiple people access our account?

Currently, each account supports one user. For team access:
- Share login credentials securely
- Or contact sales@jobplatform.com about enterprise plans with multi-user support

## Privacy and Security

### Is my data secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard security practices:
- **Encryption**: All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
- **Authentication**: Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- **Tokens**: JWT tokens with short expiration
- **Compliance**: GDPR and CCPA compliant

### What data do you collect?

**Job Seekers**:
- Email, name, phone
- Resume files
- Application history
- Search history

**Employers**:
- Email, company information
- Job postings
- Application data
- Usage analytics

See our Privacy Policy for full details.

### Do you share my data?

We never sell your data. We only share data:
- With your explicit consent
- To provide services (e.g., employers see applications)
- As required by law

### How do I delete my account?

1. Go to Profile Settings → Privacy
2. Click "Delete Account"
3. Confirm deletion

**Note**: 
- Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
- Data is deleted within 30 days
- Applications are anonymized

### Can I export my data?

Yes! GDPR data export:
1. Go to Profile Settings → Privacy
2. Click "Export Data"
3. Download JSON file with all your data

### How long do you keep my data?

- **Active accounts**: Indefinitely while account is active
- **Deleted accounts**: 30 days, then permanently deleted
- **Job postings**: Archived after 180 days
- **Applications**: Retained for 90 days after job deletion

### Is my resume secure?

Yes! Resumes are:
- Stored encrypted on secure servers (Supabase Storage)
- Only accessible to employers you applied to
- Never shared publicly
- Deleted when you delete your account

## Troubleshooting

### I can't log in

**Possible solutions**:
1. Check email and password are correct
2. Try "Forgot Password" to reset
3. Check if account is verified (check email)
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Try a different browser

### I didn't receive verification email

1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Wait 5-10 minutes (may be delayed)
3. Click "Resend Verification Email"
4. Check email address is correct
5. Contact support if still not received

### My job post isn't appearing in search

**Possible reasons**:
1. Job was just posted (wait 5 minutes for indexing)
2. Job is expired or deleted
3. Search filters exclude your job
4. Try searching by exact job title

**Solution**: Check "My Jobs" to verify job status is "Active"

### I can't upload my resume

**Possible issues**:
1. File is too large (max 5MB)
2. File format not supported (use PDF, DOC, or DOCX)
3. File is corrupted
4. Browser issue (try different browser)

**Solution**: 
- Compress PDF if too large
- Convert to PDF format
- Try different file

### Application status hasn't changed

This is normal! Employers review applications at their own pace. Most respond within 1-2 weeks, but some may take longer.

**What to do**:
- Continue applying to other jobs
- Follow up after 2 weeks if no response
- Don't wait for one application

### I'm being rate limited

You've exceeded the request limit (100 requests/minute for free tier).

**Solution**:
- Wait 1 minute and try again
- Upgrade to Basic (200/min) or Premium (500/min) for higher limits

### Search results seem wrong

**Possible issues**:
1. Filters are too restrictive
2. Typo in search query
3. No jobs match your criteria

**Solution**:
- Remove some filters
- Try broader search terms
- Check spelling

### Featured job isn't showing at top

**Possible reasons**:
1. Other featured jobs are newer
2. Job is expired
3. Search filters exclude your job

**Solution**: Featured jobs appear at top within their filter group. Check job status and filters.
