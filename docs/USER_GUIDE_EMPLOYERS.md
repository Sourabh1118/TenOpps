## Creating an Account

### Step 1: Register

1. Go to the homepage
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right
3. Select "Employer"
4. Fill in your company details:
   - Email address
   - Password (must be strong)
   - Company name
   - Company website
   - Company description
5. Click "Create Account"

### Password Requirements

Your password must have:
- At least 8 characters
- One uppercase letter
- One lowercase letter
- One number
- One special character (!, @, #, $, etc.)

**Example**: `HireNow2024!`

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile

After registration:
1. Add your company logo
2. Write a compelling company description
3. Add company size and industry
4. Verify your email address

### Company Profile Tips

**Company Description**:
- Highlight your mission and values
- Mention key products or services
- Include company culture highlights
- Keep it concise (2-3 paragraphs)

**Company Logo**:
- Use high-resolution image (min 200x200px)
- PNG or JPG format
- Square aspect ratio works best

## Posting Jobs

### Option 1: Direct Job Posting

Post jobs directly on our platform with full control over content and applications.

#### Step 1: Start a New Post

1. Go to your dashboard
2. Click "Post a Job"
3. Select "Create New Post"

#### Step 2: Fill in Job Details

**Basic Information**:
- **Job Title**: Clear, specific title (e.g., "Senior Python Developer")
- **Company Name**: Your company name (auto-filled)
- **Location**: City, state, country
- **Remote**: Toggle if position is remote

**Job Type**:
- Full-time
- Part-time
- Contract
- Freelance
- Internship
- Fellowship
- Academic

**Experience Level**:
- Entry level
- Mid level
- Senior
- Lead
- Executive

**Job Description**:
- Write a compelling description (50-5000 characters)
- Include what makes your company unique
- Describe the role and its impact
- Use bullet points for readability

**Requirements**:
- List required skills and qualifications
- Be specific but not overly restrictive
- Separate "required" from "nice to have"

**Responsibilities**:
- Outline day-to-day duties
- Describe team structure
- Mention growth opportunities

**Compensation**:
- Salary range (optional but recommended)
- Currency (USD, EUR, etc.)
- Benefits and perks

**Additional Details**:
- Tags/keywords for better searchability
- Application deadline
- Start date

#### Step 3: Review and Publish

1. Preview your job posting
2. Check for typos and errors
3. Verify all information is correct
4. Click "Publish Job"

**Note**: Publishing consumes one post from your monthly quota.

### Option 2: URL Import

Import existing job postings from other platforms.

#### Supported Platforms

- LinkedIn
- Indeed
- Naukri
- Monster
- Glassdoor

#### How to Import

1. Go to your dashboard
2. Click "Post a Job"
3. Select "Import from URL"
4. Paste the job posting URL
5. Click "Import"
6. Wait for processing (usually 1-2 minutes)
7. Review imported details
8. Edit if needed
9. Publish

#### Import Tips

**Best Practices**:
- Use direct job posting URLs (not search results)
- Verify imported information is accurate
- Add missing details before publishing
- Check that salary and requirements are correct

**Common Issues**:
- Some platforms may block automated imports
- Imported data may be incomplete
- You may need to manually add some fields

### Featured Listings

Boost visibility for important roles with featured listings.

#### What are Featured Listings?

Featured jobs:
- ⭐ Appear at the top of search results
- 🎯 Get 3-5x more views
- 💼 Stand out with special badge
- 📈 Higher application rates

#### How to Feature a Job

**Option 1: During Posting**
- Check "Feature this job" when creating post
- Consumes one featured post from quota

**Option 2: After Posting**
1. Go to "My Jobs"
2. Find the job you want to feature
3. Click "Feature" button
4. Confirm

**Note**: Featured listings last for the duration of the job posting.

## Managing Job Postings

### My Jobs Dashboard

View and manage all your job postings:

1. Go to "My Jobs" in your dashboard
2. See all jobs with status, views, and applications
3. Filter by status (Active, Expired, Filled, Deleted)

### Job Statuses

| Status | Meaning | Actions Available |
|--------|---------|-------------------|
| 🟢 Active | Currently visible to job seekers | Edit, Delete, Mark as Filled, Feature |
| ⏰ Expired | Past expiration date | Reactivate, Delete |
| ✅ Filled | Position has been filled | Reactivate, Delete |
| 🗑️ Deleted | Removed from platform | None |

### Editing a Job

1. Go to "My Jobs"
2. Click on the job you want to edit
3. Click "Edit" button
4. Make your changes
5. Click "Save Changes"

**Note**: Editing a job does not consume additional quota.

### Deleting a Job

1. Go to "My Jobs"
2. Click on the job you want to delete
3. Click "Delete" button
4. Confirm deletion

**Warning**: Deleted jobs cannot be recovered. All associated applications will be archived.

### Marking a Job as Filled

1. Go to "My Jobs"
2. Click on the filled position
3. Click "Mark as Filled"
4. Confirm

**Note**: Filled jobs are removed from search results but remain in your dashboard for reference.

### Reactivating an Expired Job

1. Go to "My Jobs"
2. Filter by "Expired"
3. Click on the job to reactivate
4. Click "Reactivate"
5. Set new expiration date
6. Confirm

**Note**: Reactivating does not consume additional quota.

## Managing Applications

**Note**: Application tracking is available on Basic and Premium tiers only.

### Viewing Applications

#### All Applications

1. Go to "Applications" in your dashboard
2. See all applications across all jobs
3. Filter by status, job, or date

#### Applications for Specific Job

1. Go to "My Jobs"
2. Click on a job
3. Click "View Applications" tab
4. See all applications for that job

### Application Details

Each application shows:
- Applicant name
- Resume (download link)
- Cover letter
- Application date
- Current status
- Your notes

### Application Statuses

| Status | When to Use |
|--------|-------------|
| 📝 Submitted | Initial status (automatic) |
| 👀 Reviewed | You've reviewed the application |
| ⭐ Shortlisted | Candidate is being considered |
| ❌ Rejected | Candidate not selected |
| ✅ Accepted | Offer extended or interview scheduled |

### Updating Application Status

1. Open an application
2. Select new status from dropdown
3. Add notes (optional but recommended)
4. Click "Update Status"

**Best Practice**: Add notes explaining your decision. This helps with future reference and team collaboration.

### Adding Notes

Use notes to:
- Record interview feedback
- Note candidate strengths/weaknesses
- Track communication history
- Share information with team members

### Downloading Resumes

1. Open an application
2. Click "Download Resume"
3. File downloads to your computer

**Tip**: Download all resumes for a job at once using "Download All" button.

### Communicating with Applicants

**Important**: Our platform does not include built-in messaging. Contact applicants using the email or phone provided in their resume.

**Best Practices**:
- Respond within 1-2 weeks
- Provide feedback when possible
- Be professional and courteous
- Keep candidates updated on timeline

## Best Practices

### Writing Effective Job Descriptions

**1. Start with a Strong Title**
- ✅ "Senior Full-Stack Developer (React/Node.js)"
- ❌ "Developer Needed"

**2. Hook Candidates Early**
- Lead with what makes your company unique
- Mention exciting projects or technologies
- Highlight growth opportunities

**3. Be Specific About Requirements**
- List must-have skills separately from nice-to-haves
- Specify years of experience needed
- Mention required certifications or degrees

**4. Describe the Role Clearly**
- What will they do day-to-day?
- Who will they work with?
- What problems will they solve?

**5. Sell Your Company**
- Company mission and values
- Team culture
- Benefits and perks
- Career development opportunities

**6. Include Compensation**
- Jobs with salary ranges get 30% more applications
- Be transparent about total compensation
- Mention benefits (health insurance, 401k, etc.)

**7. Make it Easy to Apply**
- Clear application instructions
- Reasonable requirements
- Quick application process

### Improving Application Quality

**Screen Effectively**:
- Review applications within 1-2 weeks
- Look for relevant experience
- Check for attention to detail
- Consider cultural fit

**Respond Promptly**:
- Acknowledge applications quickly
- Keep candidates informed
- Provide timeline expectations

**Provide Feedback**:
- Brief rejection notes are appreciated
- Highlight what you liked
- Suggest improvements if appropriate

### Maximizing Visibility

**Use Keywords**:
- Include relevant technologies
- Mention frameworks and tools
- Add industry-specific terms

**Post at the Right Time**:
- Monday-Wednesday mornings get most views
- Avoid posting on Friday afternoons
- Repost if not getting applications

**Feature Important Roles**:
- Use featured listings for hard-to-fill positions
- Feature senior or specialized roles
- Time features for maximum impact

**Keep Jobs Fresh**:
- Update descriptions periodically
- Reactivate expired jobs if still hiring
- Remove filled positions promptly

## FAQ

### Account Questions

**Q: Can multiple people access our employer account?**

A: Currently, each account supports one user. For team access, consider sharing login credentials securely or contact us about enterprise plans.

**Q: How do I change my company information?**

A: Go to Profile Settings → Company Information → Edit. Changes apply to all current and future job postings.

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription anytime?**

A: Yes! Cancel anytime from Subscription settings. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

### Posting Questions

**Q: How long do job postings last?**

A: 
- Free: 30 days
- Basic: 60 days
- Premium: 90 days

You can reactivate expired jobs at any time.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my monthly quota?**

A: You'll need to wait until next month or upgrade your subscription to post more jobs.

**Q: Can I edit a job after posting?**

A: Yes! Edit anytime without consuming additional quota.

**Q: Why was my URL import rejected?**

A: Common reasons:
- Domain not supported
- Invalid URL format
- Job posting no longer available
- Import quota exceeded

### Application Questions

**Q: Why can't I see applications?**

A: Application tracking requires Basic or Premium tier. Upgrade to access this feature.

**Q: How do I contact applicants?**

A: Use the contact information provided in their resume. We don't provide built-in messaging.

**Q: Can applicants see my notes?**

A: No, notes are private and only visible to you.

**Q: What happens to applications when I delete a job?**

A: Applications are archived and remain accessible in your dashboard for 90 days.

### Billing Questions

**Q: When am I charged?**

A: Subscriptions are billed monthly on the date you subscribed. You can view your next billing date in Subscription settings.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**

A: We accept all major credit cards through Stripe (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover).

**Q: Can I get a refund?**

A: We offer refunds within 7 days of subscription purchase. Contact support@jobplatform.com.

**Q: Do you offer annual billing?**

A: Yes! Annual billing saves 20%. Contact sales@jobplatform.com for details.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Can I integrate with our ATS?**

A: API integration is available for Enterprise plans. Contact sales@jobplatform.com.

**Q: Is my data secure?**

A: Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. See our Privacy Policy for details.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
